## STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS

<STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
Design Aesthetic: Polished enterprise consulting slides where structure does the persuading. Crisp geometry, generous whitespace, modular grids, and strong typographic hierarchy carry the slide. Color is surgical — a deep corporate blue for authority, a single coral accent for the one number per slide that must land. Every element is on grid; decoration is absent and rigor of layout is the only ornament.

Background:
  Texture: Flat, matte, no gradients. Pure white body slides with a thin 3–4 px IBM Blue accent rule at the top. Title and divider slides use solid Deep Navy.
  Base Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for content; Deep Navy (#0F2A4A) for title/dividers.

Typography:
  Headlines: Clean geometric humanist sans-serif at weight 600, sentence case, never all-caps. Headlines are written as findings — 6 to 14 words stating the takeaway, not the topic. Open apertures, neutral letterforms, slightly tall x-height.
  Body: Same family at weight 400 for body and weight 500 for sub-headers/labels. Sparse, left-aligned, dark slate. Numerical hero stats appear in ultra-light weight 200–300 at 60–96 pt.

Color Palette:
  Primary Text: Slate (#262626) - body copy, axis labels, table text
  Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF) - content slide background
  Section Background: Deep Navy (#0F2A4A) - title slides, dividers, hero panels
  Accent 1: IBM Blue (#0F62FE) - headers, primary chart series, callouts, top accent rule
  Accent 2: Coral Red (#DA1E28) - the one critical number/insight per slide
  Supporting: Steel Blue (#4589FF) - secondary chart series
  Tint: Pale Blue (#D0E2FF) - block fills, table header tints, highlight zones
  Secondary Text: Mid Gray (#6F6F6F) - subtitles, source notes, axis labels
  Divider: Light Gray (#E0E0E0) - 1 px rules, table grid lines

Visual Elements:
  - Thin IBM Blue rule (3–4 px) along the top edge of every content slide
  - Modular grid layouts: two-column compares, three-stat rows, layered stacks
  - Flat, unembellished charts: horizontal/vertical bars, donuts (≤4 slices), simple lines
  - Tables with pale-blue header rows and alternating white / very pale gray body rows
  - One coral element per slide: a number, a callout arrow, or a left-border on the "winning" column
  - Source lines bottom-left in mid-gray, 9–10 pt
  - Numbered stage markers (01, 02, 03…) in pale blue inside process diagram boxes
  - No icons unless they are simple monochrome line glyphs in IBM Blue or slate

Density Guidelines:
  - Content per slide: One finding headline + 2–4 supporting elements (bullets, stats, or chart). Never both a paragraph AND bullets in the same zone.
  - Whitespace: Generous margins (≥6% of slide on all sides). Whitespace between zones is the primary separator — avoid heavy dividers.

Style Rules:
  Do: Write headlines as findings not topics; group into 3s (or 4 max); reserve coral for ONE element per slide; pass the skim test; include a source line on every chart and statistic; round numbers in callouts; align every element to the underlying grid; use sentence case everywhere.
  Don't: Use gradients, drop shadows, bevels, glows, or 3D effects; mix more than two typefaces; apply more than one accent color per slide; build slides without a finding headline; crowd a slide; use clipart, emoji, hand-drawn elements, or photo backgrounds behind text; center-align body copy or bullets; use rainbow categorical palettes.
</STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
