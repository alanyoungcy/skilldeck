## STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS

<STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
Design Aesthetic: Clean, digital precision with crisp edges paired with a professional navy and gold palette. Structured grid layouts and refined whitespace project institutional credibility, executive sophistication, and contemporary business competence. Every element communicates trust, clarity, and premium quality.

Background:
  Texture: None — crisp digital clarity with pure white surfaces and subtle navy structural accents (thin rules, geometric blocks)
  Base Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF)

Typography:
  Headlines: Modern geometric sans-serif with even stroke weights, perfectly circular O shapes, and clean terminals; medium to semi-bold weight; tight but legible letter spacing; conveys contemporary business authority
  Body: Humanist sans-serif with friendly open apertures and comfortable line height; regular weight; optimized for executive scanning and reading comprehension

Color Palette:
  Primary Text: Navy (#1E3A5F) — headlines, key text, primary structural elements
  Secondary Text: Dark Gray (#4A5568) — body copy, supporting text
  Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF) — main slide background
  Accent 1: Gold (#C9A227) — premium highlights, key metrics, emphasis
  Accent 2: Light Navy (#3D5A80) — secondary elements, subheads, supporting graphics
  Neutral: Light Gray (#F3F4F6) — background sections, table fills, dividers
  Success: Corporate Green (#059669) — positive metrics
  Alert: Corporate Red (#DC2626) — attention items

Visual Elements:
  - Clean bar/line charts with thin navy axes and gold highlight series
  - Outlined professional iconography (1.5pt strokes, navy with gold accents)
  - Structured grid layouts with consistent column gutters
  - Subtle drop shadows (4px, 8% opacity) for very light depth on cards
  - Horizontal progress bars and KPI metric blocks
  - Numbered process steps and timeline graphics with navy connectors
  - Comparison tables with light gray header rows and navy text
  - Thin gold accent rules (2px) used sparingly for emphasis

Density Guidelines:
  - Content per slide: 1 headline, 1 sub-headline, 3–5 supporting points or 1 focal visual + brief caption
  - Whitespace: Generous margins (minimum 8% of slide edge); breathing room around all elements; never crowd

Style Rules:
  Do: Maintain clear visual hierarchy; use consistent grid alignment; apply gold strategically for emphasis only; keep data visualizations clean and readable; use professional outlined iconography; preserve sharp edges and precise alignment.
  Don't: Use playful, hand-drawn, or casual elements; apply heavy decorative effects or gradients; mix more than two accent colors per slide; crowd slides with information; add slide numbers, footers, or logos; use informal illustration styles.
