## STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS

<STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
Design Aesthetic: Investor-grade sports fintech aesthetic with a warm off-white textured canvas, vivid orange signal accents, and deep navy structural panels. The system feels confident, sparse, and highly directional—closer to modern financial infrastructure branding than a startup product brochure. Geometric narrative layouts use cropped corner circles and rounded navy evidence cards to create recognizable continuity without clutter.

Background:
  Texture: Warm off-white textured paper wash with very light marble/paper grain, kept calm and uniform; ambient cropped circles in pale orange or mist gray anchor corners
  Base Color: Warm Off-White (#F6F3EE)

Typography:
  Headlines: Bold, slightly editorial geometric sans-serif with confident weight and warm character (TT Chocolates style)—used oversized for cover headlines and hero numeric statements; navy with selective orange phrase emphasis
  Body: Clean, modern neo-grotesque sans-serif with controlled proportions and high legibility (TT Hoves style)—used for body copy, card text, section labels, and KPI descriptors

Color Palette:
  Primary Text: Deep Navy (#07122B) - titles, body text, connectors, high-contrast labels
  Background: Warm Off-White (#F6F3EE) - main slide canvas
  Accent 1: Gainr Orange (#F56A1C) - section labels, chevrons, highlighted phrases, metric emphasis
  Accent 2: Midnight Navy (#081632) - rounded evidence cards, KPI tiles, emphasis panels
  Soft Accent: Pale Orange (#F4C8AE) - corner circle motif
  Ambient Neutral: Mist Gray (#BFC4CC) - quiet decorative framing
  Reverse Text: White (#FFFFFF) - text inside navy cards

Visual Elements:
  - Oversized cropped corner circles in pale orange (#F4C8AE) or mist gray (#BFC4CC) for deck continuity
  - Rounded navy evidence cards (radius ~22px) with orange headings and white body copy
  - Orange chevron flow blocks for sequential or directional content
  - Thin navy connector lines with small circular nodes for grouped insight relationships
  - KPI tiles: navy rounded rectangles with oversized orange numeric callouts
  - Upper-left GAINR logo lockup as persistent orientation device
  - Section labels: short uppercase orange text above headlines

Density Guidelines:
  - Content per slide: One dominant claim plus 2-4 supporting modules; never more than one hero region
  - Whitespace: Generous outer margins (~48-72px equivalent), large gaps (~32-56px) between major regions; if content compresses, split rather than shrink

Style Rules:
  Do: Maintain warm textured background across all slides; keep orange accent disciplined as a signal color for labels, flow, and key emphasis only; build explanatory content through navy rounded cards with orange headings; preserve strong headline scale; place GAINR logo at upper-left on internal slides; use cropped corner circles quietly for continuity; lead with a sharp claim then support with compact evidence layers
  Don't: Don't use bright multicolor palettes, glossy gradients, glassmorphism, or thick borders; don't add interface-like widgets or consulting-template heavy tables; don't overuse icons—this system is typography- and geometry-driven; don't let orange compete with navy surfaces outside intentional components like chevrons or ribbons; don't crowd slides with evenly weighted text boxes
