## STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS

<STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
Design Aesthetic: Polished enterprise consulting slides where structure does the persuading. Crisp geometry, generous whitespace, modular grids, and strong typographic hierarchy carry the slide. Color is surgical — a deep corporate blue for authority, a single warm coral accent for the one number per slide that must land; charts are flat and unembellished and every element is on grid.

Background:
  Texture: Flat, matte, no gradients on body slides. Optional thin (3–4 px) accent rule at the top of each content slide in IBM Blue. Title and section dividers use Deep Navy.
  Base Color: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for content slides; Deep Navy (#0F2A4A) for title and dividers.

Typography:
  Headlines: Clean, modern humanist sans-serif at semibold weight (≈600), sentence case, never all-caps. Headlines are written as findings (6–14 words stating a takeaway), with open apertures, neutral letterforms, and a calm, authoritative presence.
  Body: Same humanist sans-serif family at regular (400) for body and medium (500) for sub-headers and labels. Dark slate text, line height 1.4–1.5. Numerical hero stats use a thin/light weight (200–300) at very large size with the unit and qualifier sitting small and quiet.

Color Palette:
  Primary Text: Slate (#262626) - body copy, axis labels, table cells
  Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF) - content slide background
  Section Background: Deep Navy (#0F2A4A) - title and section dividers
  Primary Brand: IBM Blue (#0F62FE) - headers, primary chart series, accent rule, callouts
  Supporting: Steel Blue (#4589FF) - secondary chart series
  Tint: Pale Blue (#D0E2FF) - block fills, table header tint, highlight zones
  Accent 1: Coral Red (#DA1E28) - the ONE critical number / insight per slide
  Accent 2: Teal (#007D79) - tertiary series, positive outcome callouts
  Secondary Text: Mid Gray (#6F6F6F) - subtitles, source notes
  Divider: Light Gray (#E0E0E0) - 1 px rules, table grid lines

Visual Elements:
  - Thin 3–4 px IBM Blue accent rule at top of every content slide, flush left to right margin
  - Flat, unembellished bar/line charts; primary series IBM Blue, the argued series Coral, source line in mid-gray below
  - Modular grids and rectangles in Pale Blue tint with thin IBM Blue borders for diagrams; arrows as simple 1.5 px IBM Blue lines with arrowheads
  - Hero stat blocks: thin oversized numeral in Coral, short slate rule beneath, qualifier sentence and source in small mid-gray
  - Comparison tables with Pale Blue header row, alternating white / very pale gray (#F4F4F4) body rows, a thin coral left-border on the "winning" column
  - Numbered stage badges (01, 02, 03) in Pale Blue inside boxes for process diagrams

Density Guidelines:
  - Content per slide: One finding headline, one supporting visual zone, and at most 3–4 short bullets OR one short paragraph — never both. Maximum one accent (coral) element per slide.
  - Whitespace: Generous; every element on grid; left-aligned bodies and bullets; sentence case throughout; no crowding — if it doesn't fit calmly, split into two slides.

Style Rules:
  Do: Write headlines as findings not topics; group information into 3s (4 max); reserve coral for ONE element per slide; pass the "skim test" with headline-only deck telling the story; include a source line on every chart/table/stat; round numbers in callouts (87%, 3x); align every element to the grid; use sentence case everywhere; left-align body copy.
  Don't: Use gradients, drop shadows, bevels, glows, or 3D effects; mix more than two typefaces; apply more than one accent color per slide; build slides without a finding headline; use tables as data dumps; crowd a slide; use clipart, emoji, hand-drawn elements, or photo backgrounds behind text; center-align body copy; use rainbow categorical palettes.
</STYLE_INSTRUCTIONS>
